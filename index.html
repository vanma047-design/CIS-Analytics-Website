About MISBA

Discover what we do and why you should be a part of it.

Our Mission

To provide UMD students with the resources, networking opportunities, and technical skills necessary to excel in the rapidly growing fields of Information Systems and Data Analytics.

What are MIS & BA?

MIS focuses on managing technology infrastructure to solve business problems. Business Analytics relies on data, statistical analysis, and predictive modeling to drive corporate decision-making.

The Value of Joining

Connect with corporate recruiters, attend exclusive technical workshops, build your resume through hands-on projects, and network with a community of driven students and alumni.
